# Business Insights

## 1. South Region is the Highest Revenue Generator

**Observation:** The South region generated the highest total sales among all regions.

**Data Evidence:** South recorded **$64.69M** in sales, followed by North (**$54.56M**), West (**$48.91M**), and East (**$48.86M**).

**Business Interpretation:** The South region demonstrates the strongest customer demand and market performance.

**Recommended Action:** Increase marketing investment and inventory availability in the South region while identifying strategies that can improve performance in East and West.

---

## 2. Technology is the Most Profitable Category

**Observation:** Technology is the highest profit-generating product category.

**Data Evidence:** Technology generated **$28.04M** in profit, compared to Furniture (**$3.56M**) and Office Supplies (**$1.71M**).

**Business Interpretation:** Technology products provide significantly higher profit margins than the other categories.

**Recommended Action:** Expand the Technology product portfolio and prioritize high-margin products in future sales campaigns.

---

## 3. Copiers Deliver the Highest Profit

**Observation:** Copiers are the most profitable sub-category.

**Data Evidence:** The top-performing sub-categories are:

* Copiers – **$7.31M**
* Accessories – **$7.19M**
* Phones – **$7.10M**
* Machines – **$6.45M**

**Business Interpretation:** A small number of Technology sub-categories contribute a substantial portion of overall business profit.

**Recommended Action:** Increase inventory availability and promotional focus for these high-performing products while reviewing lower-performing sub-categories.

---

## 4. Home Office Customers Generate the Highest Sales

**Observation:** The Home Office segment contributes the highest overall sales.

**Data Evidence:** Customer segment sales are:

* Home Office – **$74.50M**
* Consumer – **$71.89M**
* Corporate – **$70.63M**

**Business Interpretation:** Home Office customers represent the most valuable customer group based on revenue generation.

**Recommended Action:** Develop targeted promotions, loyalty programs, and premium product offerings specifically for Home Office customers.

---

## 5. Monthly Sales Show Seasonal Fluctuation

**Observation:** Sales are not evenly distributed throughout the year and exhibit noticeable fluctuations across months.

**Data Evidence:** The Sales Trend chart shows recurring increases and decreases rather than a stable monthly pattern.

**Business Interpretation:** Customer purchasing behaviour is influenced by seasonality, promotional activities, and market demand.

**Recommended Action:** Align inventory planning, staffing, and promotional campaigns with historically stronger sales periods while investigating causes of weaker months.

---

## 6. Higher Discounts Reduce Profitability

**Observation:** Profit generally decreases as discount percentages increase.

**Data Evidence:** The Discount vs Profit chart shows that heavily discounted orders produce lower profit compared to orders with minimal or no discount.

**Business Interpretation:** Excessive discounting erodes profit margins and reduces overall business profitability.

**Recommended Action:** Review pricing policies and restrict deep discounts to products with sufficient profit margins or strategic importance.

---

## 7. Furniture Products Experience the Highest Return Rate

**Observation:** Furniture records the highest product return rate, particularly within the Home Office customer segment.

**Data Evidence:**

* Furniture → Home Office – **9.14%**
* Furniture → Consumer – **6.95%**
* Furniture → Corporate – **6.79%**

Technology products recorded the lowest return rates, with Technology → Consumer at **2.00%**.

**Business Interpretation:** Furniture products are more likely to be returned, possibly due to shipping damage, quality concerns, or customer expectations.

**Recommended Action:** Review Furniture quality, packaging, and delivery processes while investigating the reasons behind the higher Home Office return rate.

---

## 8. Business Growth Should Focus on High-Profit Products and Strong Markets

**Observation:** Business performance is concentrated in profitable Technology products, strong regional markets, and high-value customer segments.

**Data Evidence:** Technology contributes **$28.04M** in profit, the South region generates **$64.69M** in sales, and the Home Office segment contributes **$74.50M** in revenue.

**Business Interpretation:** Concentrating resources on these high-performing areas offers the greatest opportunity for sustainable growth.

**Recommended Action:** Allocate future marketing budgets, inventory, and sales initiatives toward profitable Technology products, the South region, and Home Office customers while implementing improvement strategies for weaker-performing categories and regions.
